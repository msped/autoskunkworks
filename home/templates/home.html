What is AutoSkunkWorks?

AutoSkunkWorks exists to help you plan your current or builds you want to create.

AutoSkunkWorks aims to replace the odd pieces of paper with scribbles on and the more modern option of notes on your mobile phone. With AutoSkunkWorks we aim to give you better experience in planning your builds, from automatically updating the build total if you change the products to linking the products straight to the site where the can buy them from.

AutoSkunkWorks makes use of a simple to use dynamic interface to provide users with the best experience. Each heading has the option add various parts, which are updated regularly, in-order to ensure that you have the most up to date parts and pricing for your projected Build.

ALL Builds you create are under your control. Builds can only be seen by others if you set the visibility to Public, where they will be display on the Builds Page.

Here you can show off your next financial mistake as well as let other users vote on your build, If you have a build you want to keep a secert you can make it private.
